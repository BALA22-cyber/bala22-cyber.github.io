About Me

Hello! I am a dedicated Robotics Engineer with a Master of Science in Robotics from the University of Maryland, College Park. With a Bachelor's in Mechatronics from Sri Krishna College of Engineering and Technology, I possess a deep understanding of both mechanical systems and advanced robotics.

My journey in robotics is marked by a strong blend of theoretical knowledge and practical application, enhanced by advanced coursework in Robot Programming, Robot Perception, Deep Learning and AI, Autonomous Robotics, and Human-Robot Interaction. I am skilled in programming languages such as C++, Python, and MATLAB, and have extensive experience with robotics platforms including ROS, ROS2, and various simulation tools.

I have applied my knowledge in real-world settings with significant impact. At Oak Ridge National Laboratory, I'm actively contributing to the Real-Time Evaluator (RTE) project, enhancing precision in building component placement during crane installation. I am also working on developing Deep CuBES project where we have collected and validated Building scans using Leica MS60 and validated and created a buildings dataset for public use and for our own model training.

I am passionate about leveraging my skills to contribute to cutting-edge projects in robotics and deep learning, and I am eager to bring my strong technical background and innovative approach to new challenges in the field.
