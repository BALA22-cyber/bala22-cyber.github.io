Hello! I'm a robotics engineer and researcher with hands-on experience in developing autonomous systems, deep learning for perception, and sensor fusion. My work spans across real-time robotic applications, SLAM, and precision measurement for construction automation and including point cloud segmentation. I’ve contributed to projects at Oak Ridge National Laboratory, including automated building envelope segmentation and robotic laser tracking, and previously developed social navigation frameworks and large-scale mapping at the University of Maryland’s GAMMA Lab.

My journey in robotics is marked by a strong blend of theoretical knowledge and practical application, enhanced by experience in Robot Perception, Deep Learning, Autonomous Robot Mapping and Navigation.

I have applied my knowledge in real-world settings with significant impact. At Oak Ridge National Laboratory, I'm actively contributing to the Real-Time Evaluator (RTE) project, which uses a robotic total station, enhancing precision in building component placement during crane installation.
